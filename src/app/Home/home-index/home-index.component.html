History

I opened my clinic in 1997 with the sole purpose to help as many people as possible restore their health naturally. So, why natural health care? Well, our society has become a ‘drugged’ society – a pill for this and a pill for that hoping it will ‘cure’ whatever ails us. This doesn’t always work and sometimes the side effects can be far more damaging than the ailment. Natural health care gets to the root of your health concern rather than chasing your symptoms.
